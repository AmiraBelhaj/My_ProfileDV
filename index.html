ZINY | A hair and beauty booking plateforme

Contexte

BNP Paribas and its Moroccan subsidiary launched a start-up studio with the ambition of setting up a unique digital ecosystem in Morocco.A customer-centric approach has been conducted to understand the needs of Moroccans and 1,800 feedback have been collected through interviews, immersion sessions and focus groups.This work helped to identify and prioritize services that can improve the daily lives of Moroccans. Ziny, a hair and beauty booking plateforme in has been launched within this context

Challenge

How to ease the user beauty booking experience while helping salons to manage their business digitally on-the-go 24/7, all in one place?

Expertise provided

User Research | Service design| UX design

Design Process

Research & interviews: After Research (industry, regulatory and technology trends) and interviews (interviewing 40 potential users and 10 beauty salons) we identified 2 areas of focus. For the client: was around time management For the salon: 2 different categories of salons (the famous ones, agenda managements

Concept creation: We then involved both the client-team and clients/potential clients in co-creation sessions to validate customer insights and hypotheses (personas and pain journey) and ideate concept scenarios with them. We then prioritized those concepts and make the ideas tangible crystalized in a mock-up (service blue print) We dive deeper into the key features of the solution to challenge their relevance and feasibility as well as the sustainability of the business model with internal and external experts. We pitch the refined Concept to key stakeholders to get the venture launched and build a MVP roadmap integrating users and experts feedbacks
